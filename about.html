Integrity in Research Policy

At Evercrest University, we are committed to upholding the highest standards of integrity in all research activities. Every student, faculty member, and researcher shares the responsibility of ensuring that our discoveries are honest, transparent, and ethically sound.

Our Core Values

  • Honesty: We report data, results, methods, and procedures truthfully.
  • Accountability: We take full responsibility for the integrity of our research.
  • Respect: We acknowledge and credit the contributions of others.
  • Fairness: We engage in peer review objectively and without bias.
  • Compliance: We adhere to all ethical, legal, and institutional requirements.

Research Misconduct Includes

  • Fabrication: Making up data or results.
  • Falsification: Manipulating research materials, equipment, or processes.
  • Plagiarism: Using anotherâ€™s ideas, processes, or results without proper credit.
  • Misrepresentation: Distorting research achievements, credentials, or funding information.

Our Commitment

At Evercrest University, we promote a culture of ethical research by providing:

  • Mandatory training in Responsible Conduct of Research (RCR)
  • Transparent and fair procedures for investigating misconduct
  • Resources and mentoring for ethical scholarship
