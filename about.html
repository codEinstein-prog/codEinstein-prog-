Integrity in Research Policy

Our Core Values

  • Honesty: Report data and methods truthfully.
  • Accountability: Own your research and its impact.
  • Respect: Credit the contributions of others.
  • Fairness: Review and collaborate without bias.
  • Compliance: Follow institutional and ethical guidelines.

Research Violations

  • Fabrication: Making up data or results.
  • Falsification: Manipulating research materials or processes.

  • Misrepresentation: Distorting research outcomes or credentials.

Our Commitment

Crestmont University fosters a culture of ethical research by providing:

  • Training & education in responsible conduct of research
  • Fair investigation of reported misconduct
  • Resources for research ethics and integrity
